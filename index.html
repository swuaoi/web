About Me

My name is Aoi. My hobbies are watching Kabuki, watching airplanes, and listening to music. I often listen to classical music, kpop, and Western music. Since I was in junior high school, I have been going to see Kabuki every time my favorite actor appears. From the time I was in elementary school, I wanted to fly in the future and work in my favorite foreign language. When I was in elementary school, I met a person who became an in-house training pilot at a cram school. It became a dream to meet that senior and become a pilot. To get closer to this dream, I want to study not only Chinese and English but also a weather forecaster. I grew up in a girls' school at a middle and high school Catholic school. The important word I learned in junior high and high school is "Do what you want people to do." I want to cherish this word even in college and get involved with various friends. My dream for the future is to work for an airline since I was a kid. Through various qualifications and experiences during college life, I would like to find new dreams as well as airlines.
